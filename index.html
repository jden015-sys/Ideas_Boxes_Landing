About these resources

These Ideas Boxes are design toolkits, not scripts. Every activity is a starting point — adapt the content, context, and facilitation to fit your students and setting. The activities were developed with AI assistance and are grounded in established pedagogical frameworks, but professional judgement and knowledge of your own learners always comes first.

Each box includes filters for delivery mode, complexity, group size, and more. Click any activity card to see full instructions, facilitation notes, and watch-outs. Use the 🎲 random button when you want serendipity.
